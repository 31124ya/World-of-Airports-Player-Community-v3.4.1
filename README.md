# World of Airports (WoA) 玩家交流群 v3.4.1 管理规定
# World of Airports (WoA) Player Community v3.4.1 Regulations

---

### 一、 运行安全准则 | Member Conduct Guidelines

**1. 尊重他人 | Mutual Respect**
* 群内交流应基于互相尊重的原则，禁止使用任何形式的攻击性语言，包括但不限于人身攻击、地域歧视。
Communication should be based on mutual respect; any form of offensive language, including personal attacks or regional discrimination, is strictly prohibited.
* 禁止发布侮辱空难逝者言论，禁止滥用空难梗。
It is forbidden to post remarks insulting air disaster victims or abuse "air crash memes."

**2. 文明用语 | Civilized Language**
* 严禁发布不雅、低俗、淫秽、暴力以及具有误导性的内容。
The posting of indecent, vulgar, obscene, violent, or misleading content is strictly prohibited.
* 发言请避免重复刷屏、恶意刷图或语气不当等行为。
Please avoid spamming, malicious image flooding, or using an inappropriate tone.

**3. 禁止广告 | No Advertisements**
* 严禁一切商业性广告内容，包括但不限于推广产品、服务、私域引流等。
All commercial advertisements are strictly prohibited, including products, services, or private traffic redirection.

**4. 严禁传播违法信息 | No Illegal Content**
* 严禁传播国家法律法规禁止的内容及未经证实的谣言。
Transmission of content prohibited by laws and unverified rumors is strictly forbidden.
* **群内不得讨论或推广 VPN、翻墙工具及加速器等敏感词汇。**
**Discussion or promotion of VPNs, proxy tools, or accelerators is strictly prohibited.**

**5. 隐私保护 | Privacy Protection**
* 禁止在群内公开、传播他人隐私信息，未经本人同意不得将聊天记录转发至站外。
Forbidden to disclose others' private info; do not forward chat history externally without consent.

---

### 二、 涂装与资源管理 | Livery & Resource Management

**6. 资料保护 | Resources Protection**
* 严禁外传本群共享资源（如资料宣传、商业交易等）。
Strictly forbidden to leak shared resources (e.g., for promotion or commercial transactions).

---

### 三、 处置标准 | Disciplinary Standards

| 违规性质 Violation Type | 处理措施 Actions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **一般性违规 (General)** | 警告一次，情节反复禁言 1 天 (Warning or 1-day mute) |
| **严重违规 (Serious)** | 直接禁言或移出群聊 (Immediate mute or removal) |
| **红线违规 (Red-line)** | 永久移出群聊 (Permanent ban) |

---

### 四、 管理员规范 | Administrator Conduct

**1. 运行原则 | Operational Principles**
* 管理员执行职责时应秉持“尊重事实、快速处理、可追溯”的原则。
Admins should adhere to the principles of "fact-based, rapid processing, and traceability."

**2. 复核机制 | Review Mechanism**
* 所有处理行为须保留截图证据，管理员处理成员后需在管理组说明理由。
Screenshots must be kept as evidence; admins must explain their actions in the admin group.

**3. 控点人制度 | Point Controller System**
* 对于争议事件，由中立管理员作为“控点人”进行调查。
For disputed cases, a neutral administrator will act as a "Point Controller" to investigate.
* 控点人有权建议撤回处理决定、拉回被误踢成员或对涉事管理提出内部建议。
The Point Controller has the authority to recommend rescinding disciplinary decisions, reinstating members kicked by mistake, or providing internal suggestions to the involved administrators.
